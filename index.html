Open Source Perth Scotland

Welcome to Perth's hub for open source enthusiasts, developers, and curious minds! Whether you're a seasoned contributor or just getting started with open source, this group is your local connection to all things FOSS (Free and Open Source Software). What we're about:

  • Knowledge sharing and collaboration on open source projects
  • Regular meetups and coding sessions
  • Project showcases from local developers
  • Discussion of emerging open source technologies
  • Career opportunities in open source
  • Mentoring and support for newcomers
Join us to connect with fellow Perth developers, share your experiences, and help build a stronger open source community in Perth! All skill levels welcome.
